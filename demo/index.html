Web Development

Web development is the process of creating websites or web applications, focusing on both the user-facing design (front-end) and server-side functionality (back-end). It involves coding, structuring, and maintaining websites to ensure they are interactive, functional, and accessible.

UX/UI Desgining

UX/UI design is the process of crafting user-friendly and visually engaging interfaces for digital products. UX focuses on usability and functionality, ensuring a seamless journey for users, while UI centers on the visual aspects, like layout, colors to make interactions appealing.

App Development

App development is the process of creating software applications for mobile devices or desktops, focusing on functionality, UX, and performance. It involves coding, designing interfaces, and testing to build apps that solve specific problems or provide entertainment.
